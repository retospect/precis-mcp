A **quadrant chart** plots items on a 2×2 defined by two axes (effort vs
impact, reach vs cost, …). Reach for it when the user says "2x2", "quadrant",
"prioritization matrix", or "effort-impact matrix".

      y-axis Low Reach --> High Reach

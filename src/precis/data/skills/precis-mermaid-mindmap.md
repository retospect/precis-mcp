A **mindmap** branches a central idea into a tree of sub-topics — a
brainstorm or concept map. Reach for it when the user says "mind map",
"mindmap", "concept map", "idea tree", or "brainstorm". Indentation defines
the hierarchy.

    mindmap
      root((central idea))
        Origins
          History
        Uses
          Research
          Teaching
